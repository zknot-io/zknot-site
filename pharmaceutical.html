DEA Schedule I–V substances require documented custody from manufacturer to dispenser. ZKNOT™ makes each custody transfer event cryptographically attested and independently auditable — without a central database that becomes a single point of failure or subpoena target.

Handoff Events

What gets attested at each handoff.

Each custody transfer is one ZKKey™ button press. The chain entry contains everything a regulator or auditor needs.

Handler Identity
The ZKKey™ device ID is cryptographically bound to every entry. Each device is registered to a specific operator or facility. No anonymous transfers.
Timestamp
Set by the ZKKey™ hardware clock at the moment of button press. Cannot be backdated. Chain position provides additional temporal ordering proof.
Item Hash
SHA-256 hash of the batch record, label scan, or associated digital documentation. Proves the item state at time of transfer without exposing proprietary formulation data.
Seal Binding
TrustSeal™ ID is included in the chain entry at application. Seal removal is physically evident. Chain entry persists regardless of seal state.
Short Code
Every handoff event gets a short code printed on the TrustSeal™ or included in accompanying documentation. Any regulator can verify independently at verifyknot.io.
Chain Continuity
Each entry is mathematically linked to the prior entry. A missing or altered handoff breaks the chain — detectable by any auditor without ZKNOT™ infrastructure.
Deployment

How a pharmaceutical facility deploys ZKNOT™.

01
Register ZKKey™ devices per facility and operator role
Each ZKKey™ is registered with a device ID tied to the facility and operator role. The public key is registered at api.zknot.io. Auditors can resolve any device ID to its registration record at any time.
02
Apply TrustSeal™ at manufacture or receipt
Scan the seal barcode, press the ZKKey™ button. Chain entry is written binding the seal ID, batch documentation hash, and timestamp. Takes under 10 seconds per item or lot.
03
Attest each custody transfer with a button press
At every handoff — manufacturer to distributor, distributor to pharmacy, pharmacy to patient — the receiving handler presses the ZKKey™ button. One press. One chain entry. One timestamp. No paper form.
04
Export PDF chain-of-custody report for audit
ZK-LocalChain™ exports a signed PDF containing every handoff event in sequence, with QR codes linking to verifyknot.io for each entry. The report is itself a chain artifact — signed and independently verifiable.
