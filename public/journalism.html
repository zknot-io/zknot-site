What It Solves

The attacks on source documents
have a pattern. So does our answer.

"The document was fabricated after the fact."
The most common attack on leaked documents in litigation and press freedom cases. If you can't prove when you received the document, you can't disprove this claim.
ZKNOT™ answer: The SHA-256 hash in chain entry #19204, signed 15 days before publication, proves the document existed in its current form on February 28th.
"The journalist altered the document."
Any modification to the document changes its SHA-256 hash. A hash mismatch between the published document and the chain entry proves alteration — or disproves it.
ZKNOT™ answer: The published PDF hashes to d4e8f1a2... — identical to the value in the chain entry. The document is byte-identical to what was attested.
"Show me how you know when you received it."
Whistleblower cases, press freedom litigation, and FOIA disputes frequently turn on timeline. Without cryptographic proof, the journalist's word stands alone.
ZKNOT™ answer: The chain entry is independently verifiable at verifyknot.io — no journalist testimony required to establish the timestamp.

Workflow

From source to publication in four steps.

No specialized training required. The ZKKey™ button press takes three seconds.

01
Receive the document
Document arrives via secure drop, encrypted email, physical handoff, or any channel. The method of receipt is not recorded by ZKNOT™.
02
Attest immediately on receipt
Connect ZKKey™. The device computes a SHA-256 hash of the document. Press the physical button. A chain entry is written binding the hash and timestamp. Takes under 10 seconds.
03
Store the short code with your notes
The short code (e.g., ZK-8R2M-JT4) is your proof handle. Add it to your notes file. Print a TrustSeal™ on the physical copy if one exists.
04
Reference the chain entry in publication or legal proceedings
Include the short code and verification URL in your story notes, legal filings, or press freedom documentation. Any party can verify at verifyknot.io — no account, no call to ZKNOT™.
