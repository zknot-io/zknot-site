The ZKNOT™ Doctrine

Three layers. Zero trust required.

Competitors say "trust our features." ZKNOT™ says "verify our constraints." Every layer is independently auditable.

Layer 01
Physics Enforces
Hardware makes certain states impossible.
ZKKey™ and PowerVerify™ are built so that data exfiltration and post-nonce signing are not merely policy-prohibited — they are physically impossible by design. The hardware constraint is the guarantee.
// Constraint is structural, not behavioral
Layer 02
Math Proves
Unforgeable cryptographic records.
Every artifact is signed by a hardware-attested key and written to an append-only hash-chained ledger. The mathematics of SHA-256 and ECC make retroactive modification computationally impossible.
// Proof is mathematical, not institutional
Layer 03
You Verify
Anyone can independently confirm.
No account required. No call to ZKNOT™ needed. Any party with the short code can resolve the full chain entry at verifyknot.io — hash, device ID, timestamp, and chain position — in seconds.
// Verification is public, not permissioned
