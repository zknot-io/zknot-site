ZK-LocalChain™

Offline-capable append-only hash-chained ledger

No central authority. No internet dependency. No deletion. No edit. Every record is mathematically bound to every record before it. Any party with a single exported entry can verify the entire chain independently — without ever contacting ZKNOT™.

The chain entry format — open and auditable

Every chain entry follows a published JSON schema. Any party can parse, verify, and audit a chain export with nothing but an internet connection and a SHA-256 library.

Structural Constraints

What ZK-LocalChain™ cannot do

By design, ZK-LocalChain™ cannot:
Delete any chain entry — the append-only structure has no delete operation
Edit any prior chain entry without breaking every subsequent entry hash
Accept an unsigned entry — ZKKey™ hardware attestation is required at write time
Produce a valid chain export that conceals a gap or reordering in chain positions
Require ZKNOT™ infrastructure to verify — any exported entry is independently verifiable
