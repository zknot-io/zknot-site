What opposing counsel attacks. What ZKNOT™ eliminates.

In Court

Math and physics are the testimony.

The most effective challenge to digital forensic evidence in court is not technical — it is rhetorical. Defense counsel does not need to prove tampering occurred. They only need to introduce doubt about whether it could have.

Software-based write-blockers require the examiner to testify that the device was correctly configured, correctly operated, and free from firmware vulnerabilities. This is a human claim about a software state. It can always be questioned.

PowerVerify™ changes what you testify to. You testify that the write data lines were physically severed — a fact demonstrable with a multimeter in the courtroom. You testify that the hash in the chain entry matches the imaged drive. A fact demonstrable by any party, on any computer, with a free SHA-256 tool.

ZKNOT™ does not make your case for you. It eliminates the questions opposing counsel uses to make you prove it again.

Field Workflow

From scene to report — the complete flow.

01
Connect PowerVerify™ before touching evidence device
PowerVerify™ is inserted between the evidence device and collection laptop before any connection is made. Write path is physically severed. This is the first attestable fact in the chain.
02
Acquire with any imaging tool
Run dd, FTK Imager, Cellebrite, or your agency's standard tool. PowerVerify™ operates below the tool layer — no configuration change, no new training required.
03
Press ZKKey™ to attest the acquisition event
One button press. ZKKey™ signs a chain entry containing the artifact hash, device ID, and timestamp. Entry is written to ZK-LocalChain™. Takes three seconds.
04
Apply TrustSeal™ to physical evidence packaging
Scan the seal barcode, press ZKKey™ again. Physical packaging and digital chain entry are bound at this moment. Short code printed on seal resolves to full chain entry at verifyknot.io.
05
Attest each transfer event through the chain
Scene → lab, lab → storage, storage → court — each is one ZKKey™ press. The chain is unbroken, append-only, and independently auditable at any step by any party.
06
Export signed PDF for court or discovery production
ZK-LocalChain™ exports a signed PDF chain-of-custody report. Every entry has a QR code linking to verifyknot.io. Produce it in discovery. Opposing counsel verifies it themselves.
